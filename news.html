Q and A

  • How much can I afford?

    This depends on your income, credit history, and debts. Depending on these factors you may be able to afford enough to buy a home. This is why it is important that buyers go through pre-approval prior to viewing houses.

  • How much can I sell for?

    This depends on location, condition of property, square footage, and type of house. Agents are normally trained on providing a Comparative Market Analysis which is a value analysis of a property based on market data in the relevant area. I would love to tell you how much your house is potentially worth.

  • What sort of financing is available?

    This includes Agricultural loans, FHA Loans, Conventional loans, and VA loans. These all have varying requirements and benefits such as low to no down payment to a large down payment. Depending on what you qualify for financing may be a breeze or more difficult. The process is very detail oriented and requirement based.
